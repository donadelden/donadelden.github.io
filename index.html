Hi! I’m Denis! I received my Master’s Degree in Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Padua, Italy, in 2020. After some months spent as a research fellow, I am now a Ph.D. Student in Brain, Mind and Computer Science (BMCS) at the University of Padua. Here, I am part of the SPRITZ Security and Privacy Research Group under the supervision of Prof. Mauro Conti. Together with my academic course, I am also working with Omitech SRL as part of my high apprenticeship program. During the 2021 Summer I was granted the New Generation Internet (NGI) Explorers grant to support a collaboration with the Univeristy of Washington (Seattle, USA).
